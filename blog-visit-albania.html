Reasons to Visit Albania

Breathtaking Beaches
The Albanian Riviera is home to some of the most pristine beaches in Europe, such as Dhërmi, Ksamil, and Himara. Whether you love crystal-clear waters or picturesque coastal villages, a rental car makes it easy to explore these gems.

Historic Landmarks
Berat and Gjirokastër: UNESCO World Heritage Sites featuring stunning Ottoman architecture. Butrint National Park: Ancient ruins surrounded by lush landscapes.

Vibrant Cities
Tirana: The bustling capital offers a mix of modernity and tradition, from Skanderbeg Square to Blloku's nightlife. Shkodër: Known as the cultural capital, this city is the gateway to the breathtaking Albanian Alps.

Untouched Nature
Theth and Valbona National Parks: Perfect for hiking and connecting with nature. Lake Ohrid: One of Europe's oldest and deepest lakes, shared with North Macedonia.

Warm Hospitality
Albanians are known for their friendliness and willingness to help visitors, making every traveler feel at home.

Driving Tips for Exploring Albania
Be Prepared for Diverse Roads: While highways are well-maintained, rural roads can be challenging, requiring a reliable vehicle like those in our fleet. Watch for Local Driving Habits: Drivers may not always follow strict rules, so stay vigilant. Fuel Availability: Gas stations are plentiful near cities and towns, but they may be sparse in rural areas.
