To rent a car from A.S Kasalla, you must be at least 21 years old (age may vary for certain vehicles), have a valid driver's license, and provide a form of payment, such as a credit card or PayPal account.

While insurance is not mandatory to rent a car, we recommend it for your peace of mind. We offer various insurance options during the booking process.

We operate on a full-to-full fuel policy, meaning you should return the vehicle with the same amount of fuel as when you picked it up. Failure to do so may incur a refueling charge.

You can modify or cancel your reservation through our website or by contacting our customer support team. Please check our cancellation policy for specific terms.

Booking a car is easy! Simply visit our website, select your desired vehicle, choose your rental dates, and follow the prompts to complete your reservation.

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options. Please see our website for a full list of accepted payments.

Additional fees may apply for extras such as GPS navigation, child seats, or additional drivers. Please review your rental agreement for full details.
